Surveillance Capitalism:
The drive toward more and more data extraction and analysis, development of new contractual forms using computer-monitoring and automation, the desire to personalize and customize the services offered to users of digital platforms, the use of the techno-logical infrastructure

to carry out continual experiments on its users and consumers.
